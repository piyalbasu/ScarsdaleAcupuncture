My doctor then recommended Andrea. I knew immediately after meeting her that I had found the right person. She reviewed my history, explained the treatments, was in touch with my doctor and from the very beginning I knew I was in good hands. Andrea is not only well studied in the treatment of infertility, but has a caring and gentle spirit that can only help in making her patients feel confident and at ease. After about two months of being treated by Andrea, we went ahead with IVF, even though the drugs hadn't stimulated adequate egg production for me in the past.
