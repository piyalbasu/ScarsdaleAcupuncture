My husband Jeff and I assumed like all other couples in their mid thirties that we would get pregnant immediately. After several thousand dollars with an attempt at IUI and four failed IVF’s I had reconciled with the idea that I would never be able to bear children. I was referred to you by my friend, Robin Ordan, LCSW, LMT, after four long years of infertility. Last year around the same time I walked into your office defeated and hopeless. I had given up all hope but something told me to exhaust all possible means. So with a little optimism and your caring bedside manner I was on my way to success! From the initial consultation I could tell the concern and compassion you held for your patients. I could not believe the depth and proficiency in which you examined my medical history. You addressed concerns that were never discussed by prior medical professionals. I started to feel Zen-like and calmer after each visit with you. The office visits were peaceful and I started looking forward to the next one. Unbeknownst to me, each appointment was a stepping stone towards my quest for motherhood. After a month and a half of treatments (acupuncture twice/week and herbs daily), I got pregnant! At first I couldn’t believe it when the doctor told me the incredible news. Our beautiful baby son Gage was born on January 24, 2008. He was a very healthy 8.5lbs! We just celebrated his first year with us. There is not one day that goes by that I don’t think of you and the miracle you blessed me with. Motherhood is everything I had hoped for with my son. I can’t thank you enough for your compassion and kindness throughout my treatments with you. I am forever grateful for all that you have done for me. I hope you will share my story with your prospective patients.

Carolina, Port Chester, New York

Gage Taren was born on January 24, 2008 weighing 8lbs. 5oz.
