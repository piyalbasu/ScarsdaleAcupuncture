Scarsdale Acupuncture

Licensed Acupuncturists and Herbalists

In the spring of 2004, I was diagnosed with Poly Cystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) by a fertility specialist. After several cycles of Clomid and one IUI, I conceived my daughter. One year and a half later, my husband and I attempted to conceive again using Assisted Reproductive Therapy. After five failed Clomid and IUI cycles, the fertility specialist added Secondary Unexplained Infertility to my diagnosis. He explained that the next course of action would need to be IVF. This was not covered by our health insurance and we were not prepared financially to pursue it on our own. In addition, we were uncomfortable with the increased odds for multiple births. During this time, I began to hear of and read about acupuncture as a means to successfully achieve pregnancy. I began my treatment with Andrea in May of 2007. I was immediately impressed by her knowledge of acupuncture and its uses to treat infertility. Throughout my treatment with Andrea, I came to regard her as a kindred spirit who carefully considered my emotional and psychological well being as we traveled this journey together. Andrea recommended that I incorporate Traditional Chinese Medicine in the form of herbal teas to help correct my body’s imbalances and deficits in addition to my acupuncture treatments. She also suggested other natural strategies to improve my chances for conception. After four months of regular acupuncture treatments in conjunction with the consumption of my herbal tea prescription and small lifestyle changes, my husband and I conceived our second daughter naturally! In addition, I enjoyed a dramatic overall improvement in physical health and sense of well being. Andrea is a highly talented and compassionate health care professional.

Cindy, Pelham, New York
