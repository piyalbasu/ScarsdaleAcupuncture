Last December I had a miscarriage, not knowing when I actually got pregnant because my periods were so irregular. After a couple failed attempts with Clomid and continued irregular cycles, a friend of mine suggested acupuncture to increase my chances of conception. I did some research and thought, “Why not give it a shot”? I was very careful in choosing an Acupuncturist-- I really wanted someone with whom I could communicate and feel comfortable with during this delicate time in my life. When I met Andrea for the first time, I felt totally comfortable; she really made me feel at ease and is quite knowledgeable with the world of infertility. The first two months I tried Clomid in conjunction with acupuncture. Again, no success. Andrea really wanted me to be Clomid-free for three months so that we could regulate my cycle. My cycle was regulated after one month, which was a miracle to me, as I never had a regular cycle. My plan was to start IVF after the third month of being free from Clomid. Well, I was about to pick up my prescriptions for my first IVF cycle when I found out I was pregnant!  I know that my pregnancy is due to the work I did with Andrea. I really couldn't have asked for a more compassionate, knowledgeable, helpful caregiver. I am now seven weeks pregnant and plan on continuing acupuncture for the remainder of my pregnancy. It truly helps me relax, and I even got use to the needles. Andrea, thank you from the bottom of my heart, I truly am blessed to have you in my life during these challenging times. 

Frances, Bronxville, New York
